## Locked decisions

- **D-06-transport:** Separation via envelope `type`; relay filters/routes on semantics, not transport-level channel split.
- **D-01:** Envelope includes `type`, `space_id`, `thread_id`, `schema_version`, `message_id`, etc.

## Routing constants (concrete wire contract for this plan)

- Route key prefix literal: `talkie:v1`
- Control segment literal: `control`
- Conversation segment literal: `conversation`
- Control route key format: `talkie:v1:control:${space_id}` (single colon separators, no slashes).
- Conversation route key format: `talkie:v1:conversation:${space_id}:${thread_id}`.
